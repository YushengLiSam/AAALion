**Decision needed**: hand-curate (more credible) OR expand AI-gen (more recall)?

### B. Cache strategy

**Status**: `services/cache.py` written — 200-entry LRU, 10-min TTL, keyed on hash(system_prompt + messages + image_sha).

**My preference**: wire it as-is. 10 min is short enough that judges won't see stale answers; long enough to make repeat demo queries snap.

**Alternative**: longer TTL with explicit invalidation rules. Or no cache (avoid "did the model actually answer or just replay?" optics).

**Decision needed**: 10-min LRU (my default) OR longer TTL OR skip?
